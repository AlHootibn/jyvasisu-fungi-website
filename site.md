# Website Structure: JyväSisu Fungi

## Navigation
- **Home** — Hero section featuring fresh, locally grown mushrooms from JyväSisu Fungi
- **Our Varieties** — Catalog of specialty mushrooms (Oyster, Lion's Mane, Shiitake)
- **Sustainability** — Explanation of the local, eco-friendly farming process in Finland
- **Where to Buy** — List of local markets in Jyväskylä and direct order options
- **Contact** — Order form and contact information

---

## Page Breakdown

### Home (`/`)
**Hero**
- Full-bleed image: fresh harvested mushrooms or cultivation room
- Headline: *"Fresh from the Forest to Your Table — Locally Grown in Jyväskylä."*
- Subline: one sentence on sustainable, small-batch urban farming
- CTA: "See Our Varieties" → `/varieties`

**About strip**
- Brief paragraph on the farm's origin story: transition from conventional to sustainable urban farming
- Emphasis on local roots in Jyväskylä, Central Finland
- Supporting stat or milestone (e.g., Founded in 2026, kg harvested per month)

**Featured Varieties**
- 3 cards: Oyster, Lion's Mane, Shiitake
- Each: photo, name, one-line flavor note
- Link: "Explore All Varieties"

**Gallery teaser**
- 4–6 image grid: cultivation process, spore inoculation, harvest, packaging
- Link: "See the Process"

**Where to Find Us (preview)**
- Short intro + 2–3 market/shop logos or names
- CTA: "See all stockists" → `/where-to-buy`

---

### Our Varieties (`/varieties`)
**Intro**
- Short paragraph on the farm's cultivation approach (substrate, controlled environment, no chemicals)

**Variety catalog**
Each entry includes:
- Large photo
- Finnish name + Latin species name (in monospace)
- Flavor profile (e.g., "Mild, velvety, earthy")
- Best uses (e.g., "Risotto, stir fry, soups")
- Availability: year-round or seasonal badge

Varieties:
1. **Oyster Mushroom** (Osterivinokas — *Pleurotus ostreatus*)
2. **Lion's Mane** (Leijonanharjasieni — *Hericium erinaceus*)
3. **Shiitake** (*Lentinula edodes*)

**Process note**
- Short illustrated explainer of how mushrooms move from spore to shelf

---

### Sustainability (`/sustainability`)
**Philosophy**
- Connection to Finnish foraging tradition and forest culture
- How indoor cultivation reduces pressure on wild mushroom populations
- Commitment to chemical-free, traceable growing

**Practices (3-column layout)**
1. Local substrate sourcing (straw, wood chips, coffee grounds)
2. Zero-waste compost loop — spent substrate returned to soil
3. Minimal packaging, compostable materials

**Environmental note**
- Brief comparison: indoor farm vs. wild harvest impact
- Reference to Jyväskylä's local food ecosystem

---

### Where to Buy (`/where-to-buy`)
**Local markets**
- List of Jyväskylä markets, farm shops, or grocery partners
- Market day + hours for each
- Map embed (OpenStreetMap or Google Maps)

**Direct orders**
- Option to order fresh mushrooms directly from the farm
- Simple form or email CTA for home delivery / pickup

**Wholesale & Restaurants**
- Short callout for chefs and buyers
- Dedicated contact email or form

---

### Contact (`/contact`)
**Two-column layout**
- Left: contact form (name, email, subject dropdown: General / Order / Wholesale / Visit, message)
- Right: farm address in Jyväskylä, phone, email, operating hours

**Social**
- Instagram and Facebook links

**Map**
- Embedded location map for the farm

## Content Tone
- Warm, direct, Finnish — no marketing fluff
- Bilingual ready: Finnish primary, English secondary
- Sensory-first product descriptions: smell, texture, taste before use case
